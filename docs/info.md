# StepUpIntern Backend - Project Overview

## 1. Project Summary

StepUpIntern is an AI-powered Internship and Career Development Platform designed to connect students, recruiters, educational institutions, and industry partners through a unified ecosystem.

The platform helps students discover internship opportunities, improve career readiness, analyze resumes, identify skill gaps, participate in assessments, and receive AI-driven career guidance.

Recruiters can create internship opportunities, manage applicants, schedule interviews, and hire qualified candidates through an intelligent recruitment workflow.

Administrators oversee platform operations, company verification, user management, content moderation, analytics, and overall system health.

---

## 2. Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing students with career development tools while enabling recruiters to efficiently discover and hire suitable talent.

---

## 3. Target Users

### Students

Students seeking internships, career guidance, skill development opportunities, freelance projects, and professional growth.

### Recruiters

Companies, startups, and hiring managers looking to publish internships, evaluate candidates, manage interviews, and recruit talent.

### Administrators

Platform managers responsible for moderation, verification, analytics, support, and operational management.
